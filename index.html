Our Story

Founded in the heart of tradition, 和茶庵 (Wa-cha-an) embodies the timeless art of Japanese tea ceremony. Our name combines "wa" (harmony), "cha" (tea), and "an" (hermitage), reflecting our commitment to creating a peaceful sanctuary where guests can experience the profound beauty of tea culture.

Each cup we serve carries centuries of wisdom, carefully prepared using traditional methods passed down through generations. We believe that tea is not merely a beverage, but a pathway to mindfulness and inner peace.

Our Philosophy

和 - Wa (Harmony)

Creating peaceful connections between people and nature through tea

敬 - Kei (Respect)

Honoring the tea, the ceremony, and each guest with deep reverence

清 - Sei (Purity)

Maintaining cleanliness of heart, mind, and environment

寂 - Jaku (Tranquility)

Finding inner peace through the meditative practice of tea
